  HOW TO COMMIT IN CURSOR - SUPER SIMPLE GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: OPEN SOURCE CONTROL
────────────────────────────
• Look at the LEFT side of Cursor window
• See a list of icons? Find the one with a BRANCH/TREE symbol
• Click on it

OR press: Ctrl + Shift + G (all at once)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 2: YOU SHOULD SEE A PANEL
───────────────────────────────
After clicking, you'll see a panel that shows:
• "Source Control" at the top
• List of files below (your new deployment files)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 3: ADD ALL FILES
──────────────────────
• Look for a "+" button (plus sign)
• It might say "Stage All Changes" next to it
• CLICK THE "+" button

This adds all your files to be committed.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 4: TYPE YOUR MESSAGE
──────────────────────────
• Look for a text box at the top that says "Message"
• Click in that box
• Type exactly: Add deployment configuration

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 5: COMMIT
──────────────
• Look for a CHECKMARK button (✓) or "Commit" button
• Click it

OR press: Ctrl + Enter

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 6: PUSH TO GITHUB
───────────────────────
• Look for three dots (...) at the top
• Click the three dots
• Click "Push" or "Push to..."

OR look for a cloud/upload icon and click it

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

DONE! ✅
────────
Your files are now on GitHub!

Check here: https://github.com/belgarathe/pactattack
